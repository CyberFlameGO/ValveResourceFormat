VRF / Source 2 Resource Viewer

This program can open Valve's VPK packages and most Source 2 assets (maps, models, meshes, materials, textures, sounds, and more!)

All Source 2 projects are supported: Dota 2, Half-Life: Alyx, SteamVR, Underlords, and others
